WE ARE XYD

XYD 713 LABORATORY SERVES AS A MAJOR RESOURCE FOR INSPIRATION, EDUCATION, COLLABORATION, ENJOYMENT AND CULTURAL INTERESTS OF THE xCOMMUNITY

Blank goods are ready and the pre-press process finished. The idea is shaped and transformed into digital illustration, color separations are made, films are printed and screens are burned and prepared

We don’t just screen print on garment. We also screen print on paper bags, totes, vinyls, towels as well as lighters and skateboard decks. If there is something specific, don’t hesitate to contact us

If you’re starting a brand, printing your annual conference tees, creating some promotion materials we can cater to your needs. Let us do the prep and all the dirty job, so that you can focus on the fun part

Our screen printing story began in the middle of the desert, in Tel Aviv in early 2016. XYD started small, offering creative screen printing over socks and t-shirts within a small apartment at south of the city. In middle 2017, we expanded, doubling our capacity, upgrading our printing equipment, decided to make an impact on our native surroundings, so we moved to Kharkov. Our first official contemporary lab reveal itself on 120sq. m at the basement floor of the 18th century building. In 2018 we expand again, opening second laboratory in Kiev already. XYD is always about an art. We’ve spent the past five years cultivating skills in digital design and screen printing, every time seeking out better options for fabric, supplies and garment designs.
